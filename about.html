Overview

Gamer Grub Hub is all about what to eat while you have better things to do, like slaying dragons, or exploring the galaxy. Who has time to cook for themselves when they would rather be spending their free time cooling off after a hard day at work, school, or just having nothing better to do. At Gamer Grub Hub we talk about some of the food essentials you will need while undergoing extended gamming sessions, from the best snacks and sodas, and anything else you might need. We also talk about some of the best things to bring to the table whenver you are playing some DnD with your friends. Or any other table top system out there. We all know that snakcs are some of the best parts of comming over, so its important to bring the right goodies.

Storyline

Gamer Grub Hub was started in March 2020, with the goal of keeping the gamers of the world fed. Most of us probably don't know much about cooking. Since then we have continued to work on bringing you content on all the latest food ideas for gamers.

Vision

As people who do not really care that much about eating, and would rather be spending time gaming, our food blog will be about the best things to get for prolonged gaming sessions. By detailing articles on the essential snack food for gamers.
